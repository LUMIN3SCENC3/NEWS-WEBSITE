TECNO Camon 50 Pro focused on imaging

Technology | February 19, 2026 10:29am | by JULIUS TABIOS

TECNO continues its camera-centric midrange push with the Camon 50 series. The Camon 50 Pro lands as the higher tier variant, leaning on imaging hardware, a large battery, and a fast panel rather than trying to compete purely on raw flagship performance.

DISPLAY - 6.78-inch curved AMOLED - 1.5K resolution (1208 × 2644) - Up to 144Hz refresh rate

PERFORMANCE - MediaTek Helio G200 Ultimate - Up to 12GB RAM + virtual RAM expansion - Up to 256GB storage

CAMERA - 50MP main camera (1/1.56″ sensor, OIS) - 50MP 3x telephoto - 8MP ultra-wide - 32MP front camera

BATTERY - 6,150mAh battery - 45W fast charging

OTHER FEATURES - HiOS 16 (Android 16) - Dual speakers with Dolby Atmos - NFC, IR blaster, USB-C - Multiple color options

The Camon 50 Pro is positioned as the imaging-focused member of the lineup while keeping endurance and display smoothness competitive in the midrange space. Availability has begun in select markets with pricing varying per region, with broader rollout expected after its global showcase.
