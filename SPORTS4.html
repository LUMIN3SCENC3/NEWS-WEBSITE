Inna Palacios embraces life after national team retirement

Football | February 18, 2026 7:51pm | by ROMMEL FUERTES JR.

Former Filipinas goalkeeper Inna Palacios attended the 2026 Philippine Sportswriters Association Awards Night at Manila Diamond Hotel on Monday with a new focus: life after the national team.

For the first time in years, Palacios appeared at a sporting event not as a member of the Philippine women’s national football team.

“I’m enjoying my time away from the team now,” she said before receiving a special award for the Filipinas’ gold medal run in the 2025 Southeast Asian Games.

“I’m just trying to find other ways to grow in sports aside from playing. I’m still playing in the local league, I still love it but right now, I think I’ve given enough for the national team. I know I have, so I’m finding other ways to grow as a person.”

After helping the Filipinas win the gold in Thailand a few months ago, Palacios quickly announced her retirement from the national squad.

Stepping away from the national team doesn’t mean she’s leaving the sport. She remains involved with Kaya FC, though the national team is another matter.

“I think 18 years was enough. I’ve seen the national team grow from nothing into something bigger. Right now, it’s all about the future.”
