HUAWEI unveils latest foldable phone, PaperMatte tablet, and upgraded ear buds

Technology | February 21, 2026 6:13pm | by JEM SAGCAL

HUAWEI introduces the Mate X7, MatePad 11.5 S 2026, and the Free Clip 2 in their exclusive Flagship Product Launch, February 19, 2026.

Fashion meets innovation as HUAWEI’s latest product line combines style and functionality for everyday use in work or leisure. HUAWEI’s latest foldable phone, the Mate X7 features a soft vegan leather exterior and boasts a durable and water resistant structure.

The Mate X7’s most notable inclusion is its 50mp telephoto macro camera and immersive dual screen feature. Using its foldable screen, two windows can be opened simultaneously to allow seamless multitasking.

“Performance and reliability to new heights without compromise,” describes Product Expert and Presenter, Gino Avena. The Huawei Mate X7 is priced at P119,999.

Memory: 16 GB RAM + 512 GB ROm

50 MP Ultra Lightning HDR Rear Camera, F1.49-F4.0 with 40 MP Ultra-Wide Angle Camera, F2.2, and a 50 MP Telephoto Macro Camera, F2.2

8 MP Front Camera, 5600 mAh batter, Splash, water, and dust resistant rate at IP58 and IP59

Introducing the HUAWEI MatePad 11.5 S 2026

The HUAWEI MatePad 11.5 S 2026 PaperMatte Edition is a 4-in-1, ultra thin tablet that comes with a detachable Smart Keyboard and Huawei M-Pencil. This year’s edition is 515g light and 6.1mm thin.The MatePad highlights AI companions in note taking and sketch that targets students and on-the-go professionals. Multi-tasking is also possible with the floating window.

The tablet is priced at P39,999, but will go on sale at P29,999 for the month.

Specifications: Memory: 12 GB ROM: 265 GB / 512 GB 12 MP Rear Camera, F/1.8 8 MP Front Camera, F/2.0 8800 mAh battery 10v/4A standard charger

A more comfortable, light, and soft ear buds Besides their devices is the next line of their innovative earbuds, the HUAWEI FreeClip 2. The latest design is made to be 25% softer and 14% lighter. The FreeClip 2 can be bought for P8,999.
Products are now available at HUAWEI’s official website.
