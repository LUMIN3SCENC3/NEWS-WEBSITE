Meet the Ultimate 8 of 'Pinoy Big Brother: Celebrity Collab 2.0'

Entertainment | February 21, 2026 7:19pm | by JADE VERONIQUE YAP

The Ultimate 8 of "Pinoy Big Brother: Celebrity Collab 2.0" is now complete!
Following the eviction of Marco Masa and Carmelle Collado on Saturday, the remaining eight housemates are now set to advance to the Big Night on February 28.

The Ultimate 8 was determined through two big jump challenges, which were won by Lella Ford and Krystal Mejes for Kapamilya, and Caprice Cayetano and Ashley Sarmiento for Kapuso.

The four remaining spots were won by Miguel Vergara, Heath Jornales, Princess Aliyah, and Joaquin Arce through public voting during the season’s final eviction night.
